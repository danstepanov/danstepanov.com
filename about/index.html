About Me

I'm a technical product manager coming from a background of both software and chemical engineering. When I'm not working, I'm either at hackathons, reading something informative, trying to decide which motorcycle I'm getting (probably a Triumph Bonneville), or combatting this damn existential dread. I currenlty live in LA but I tend to spend a lot of time in NY.

I graduated with a degree in Chemical Engineering from Penn State in May of 2015 but I discovered my passion for coding and product development at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. During one weekend in September of 2014, my entire worldview was shattered and I decided to learn as much about coding as I possibly could. I even ended up writing code to do my homework at Penn State while trying to spend more and more time with my hackathon buddies in Michigan.

I'm currently reading Ghost in the Wires by Kevin Mitnick and Mastering the Market Cycle by Howard Marks. I highly recommend both! I enjoy a good game of chess and other cerebral games like Avalon.

Ask me things on Twitter if you're curious.
